## About

`LeanTopology` formalizes a sequence of introductory topology topics in Lean, including topological spaces, neighborhood systems, bases and subbases, convergence, and closure-related constructions.

The blueprint is written in a mathematically natural style, with LaTeX-heavy statements and detailed proof sketches aligned with the formal declarations in the repository.
